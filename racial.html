Race

Race has become a very charged topic in the media, especially with the normalization of racism by major political figures. More often than not, all women, regardless of race, are held up to a false standard of what they should be, or even just get generalized based on their race. In regards to women, not only are they held to sexist standards, but they also have the burden of having many racial stereotypes placed onto them.
